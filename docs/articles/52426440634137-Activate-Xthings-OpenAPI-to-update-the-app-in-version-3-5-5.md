# Activate Xthings OpenAPI to update the app in version 3.5.5

> Source: https://developer.xthings.com/hc/en-us/articles/52426440634137-Activate-Xthings-OpenAPI-to-update-the-app-in-version-3-5-5

---
**To access the Xthings OpenAPI or API credentials, update your Xthings Home(Formerly U home) App to version 3.5.5. Once the update is complete, you can enable the API directly within the App. Please follow the step-by-step instructions with screenshots below:**

Open the Xthings Home App version 3.5.5 -> Go to My account -> Check at the bottom OpenAPI -> Click on Next -> Choose your role -> Select your device -> click on Activate OpenAPI -> Save it

Congratulations! You have successfully activated the Xthings OpenAPI. Thank you.
