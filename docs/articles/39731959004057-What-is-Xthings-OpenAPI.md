# What is Xthings OpenAPI

> Source: https://developer.xthings.com/hc/en-us/articles/39731959004057-What-is-Xthings-OpenAPI

---
The Xthings OpenAPI enables your application to quickly connect to U-tec-related devices, including WiFi locks, smart plugs, smart switches, and lights. After integrating the Xthings OpenAPI, you can interact with U-tec smart devices within your application, handle device status events, and communicate commands.
